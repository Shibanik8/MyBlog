Manali, nestled in the heart of Himachal Pradesh, is a beloved mountain escape known for its snow-capped peaks, lush valleys, and adventurous spirit. Whether you're strolling through the lively streets of Old Manali, feeling the chill at Solang Valley, or marveling at the beauty of Rohtang Pass, the town offers a perfect mix of serenity and thrill. From cozy cafes and Tibetan monasteries to river rafting and trekking trails, Manali caters to both peace-seekers and adrenaline lovers.
